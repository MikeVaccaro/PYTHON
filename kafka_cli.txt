Can reset offsets by/to:
    --shift-by <positive or negative integer>
     --to-current
    --to-latest
    --to-offset <offset_num>
    --to-datetime <datetime_string>

--to-datetime note: time zone is assumed to be UTC if it's not mentioned in the datetime_string
also can do on a particlar partition
--topic someTopicName:0
